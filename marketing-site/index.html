Questions

The honest answers.

Is this just ChatGPT for deals?

No. The valuation, EBITDA, DSCR and funding maths are computed by a deterministic engine, not generated by a language model. The AI reads documents and writes the words around the numbers, but it can't invent a figure.

Will the emails read like AI wrote them?

No, and we're strict about it. Every draft is written to read like you wrote it, with no AI tells, no filler and nothing you'd be embarrassed to send. You review and send.

Do I have to learn prompts?

No. You move a deal across your board and the right specialist does the work for that stage. There's nothing to prompt.

Whose methodology does it use?

Yours. It starts from a proven acquisition framework and you tune the buy box, sectors, return targets and exclusions to how you actually invest.

Can my whole team use it?

Yes. The Team plan gives shared pipeline and CRM, multiple mandates and roles, so everyone works the same deals with the same brain behind them.
